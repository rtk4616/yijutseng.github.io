About Me

Yi-Ju earned a PhD degree in Biomedical Electronics and Bioinformatics from National Taiwan University in 2013. She is a postdoctoral research fellow in Computational Health Informatics Program, Boston Children’s Hospital, Boston, MA, USA. Her research interests include medical informatics, clinical decision support, and infection surveillance.
